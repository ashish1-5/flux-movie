How JWT works in Flux Movie:
Tokens are signed with HMAC-SHA256 using a secret key. The payload contains user email, name, role, and expiry (iat/exp). The signature prevents tampering — any change to header or payload invalidates the token. Tokens expire after 7 days. In a real backend, the server verifies the signature on every protected route.

🎬 Our Story

About Flux Movie

One platform. Every movie.

🎬 All Movies at One Place

Stop switching between apps. Flux Movie brings every Hindi and English movie — Bollywood blockbusters, Hollywood hits, indie gems, and classics — all under one roof. Search once, find everything.

💸 Compare Prices Before You Pay

Why pay more? Flux Movie shows you every platform where a movie is available — Netflix, Prime Video, JioCinema, Hotstar, SonyLIV and more — along with their prices in Indian Rupees. Pick the cheapest option instantly.

📺 Free Movies — We Find Them for You

Many great movies are available completely free on YouTube and ad-supported platforms. Flux Movie highlights them first so you never pay for something you can watch for free.

What we offer

  • 🎬 Official trailers & free full movies on YouTube
  • 💰 Price comparison in ₹ across all OTT platforms
  • 🔥 Daily trending charts — Hindi & English
  • ✨ Latest 2025–2026 releases updated regularly
  • ⭐ IMDb top-rated picks curated by rating
  • 🌐 Streaming availability — Netflix, Prime, Hotstar & more
  • ❤️ Personal wishlist to save movies for later
  • 🔍 Search across thousands of Hindi & English titles

Powered by

Flux Movie uses the OMDB API for movie data and IMDb ratings, YouTube Data API for trailers and free movies, and Watchmode API for real-time streaming availability and pricing. All prices are converted to Indian Rupees (₹).
